About LevanV

We believe in a Happy nation which progresses economically — and this thought process paved a path to “Levan Ventures”— a platform where we carry out the business of leadership development program which increases employee morale and retention, improve productivity, promote better decision making, build better teams, and train future leaders in a company to has a management style that is appropriate to a positive working atmosphere.

We organize Podcasts, e-Magazine, and an online Talkz program and successful business ventures that create jobs and wealth in the region, along with encouraging an attitude of innovation in the country as a whole.

We carry out the business of developing, promoting, and marketing. We organize business of promotional events, Award ceremonies, Product launching platform and to carry out Technical & non-technical Summits, Workshops & Seminars.

All these are the solutions we devised in our quest to find what will be an effective way to serve our nation and its upcoming generations to become prosperous and economically progressing. We believe that work needs to be done from the base pillars if we want the outcome to be beautiful. And we believe that students are those pillars and shaping them into better citizens is the first step towards realizing our vision.

"We are here to make a difference — a good one"
